Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Effective date: July 2025

1. Introduction

StorageNinja ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles information when you use our service. StorageNinja is a browser extension designed to help developers and users manage browser storage data including Local Storage, Session Storage, and Cookies.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data We Do NOT Collect

StorageNinja does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information or identifiable data
  • Your browsing history or website visits
  • Storage data from your browser (Local Storage, Session Storage, Cookies)
  • Any content or data from websites you visit
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information or system details

2.2 Local Processing Only

All data processing occurs locally on your device. When you use StorageNinja to view, copy, paste, or modify storage data, these operations happen entirely within your browser. No data leaves your device or is sent to external servers.

3. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use, share, or process any personal data. StorageNinja operates as a purely local tool that:

  • Accesses browser storage APIs only when explicitly requested by the user
  • Processes data locally within your browser environment
  • Does not store any data between sessions
  • Does not communicate with external servers

4. Permissions Explained

StorageNinja requests the following Chrome permissions for functionality:

Required Permissions:

  • activeTab: Access the currently active tab only to read storage data when the extension popup is opened by the user
  • storage: Access Chrome's storage API for extension settings and preferences
  • cookies: Read and modify cookies on the currently active tab only (limited by activeTab permission)
  • scripting: Dynamically inject content scripts into the active tab only when needed to access Local Storage and Session Storage
  • clipboardRead: Read clipboard data for paste functionality
  • clipboardWrite: Write storage data to clipboard for copy functionality

Enhanced Security Approach

StorageNinja uses only activeTab permission - no broad website access required! The extension can only access storage data from your currently active tab and only when you explicitly open the extension popup. This minimal permission approach ensures maximum privacy and security.

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and not for data collection or tracking purposes. The extension operates with minimal permissions to ensure your privacy and security.

5. Data Security

Since all data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Your data never leaves your browser environment
  • No network transmission of sensitive information occurs
  • Storage data remains under your complete control
  • The extension cannot access data from other extensions or browser components

6. Third-Party Services

StorageNinja does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. The extension operates entirely offline and independently.

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data collection occurs, COPPA compliance concerns do not apply to our service.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last modified" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
